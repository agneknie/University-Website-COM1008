Flexi Space

When the strict forms of boxes in COM1008 seems like an unescapable maze visit this cornerless space. Freedom of the mind and an inspiration for creativity is guaranteed.

Group Study

No better place to argue with your groupmates over user stories for COM1001 Requirements Document. Plan your stay well in advance though – Group Studies are a hot commodity and booking in advance is required. Book your place now by clicking here.

Big Silent Study

A Silent Study for a true Computer Scientist – multiple computers are available for focused and productive study session or for uninterrupted procrastination in silence.

Small Silent Studies

Brag to your friends about the prestigious University you are studying in – the best shots of Sheffield are taken through the windows of these two Silent Studies. Other options include studying for COM1002 whilst looking at St George's Church.

Short-term Silent Study

A great place to kill time productively in between lectures or to catch a short, last minute revision before that COM1003 Java test.

Media Booths and TV Studio

Start a vlogger career, edit videos of your flatmates or produce a full-blown documentary about the 4th Floor of The Diamond – the Media Booths and the TV Studio have all the tools you could possibly need. But until you become a mega-star booking in advance is required - click here to do just that.

Library and CiCS Help Service

As Computer Scientists we do not want to admit it but it is true – sometimes even the best of us need some technological help. Do not be a silly stud and drop by if you are stuck setting up your University VPN, have some Blackboard problems or just forgot your password.

Library Reference Collection

Although the Internet is considered to be God by all Computer Scientists, books are still very helpful too. Drop by and pick up a valuable piece of literature yourself. But if you’re too lazy to climb all those steps check out the online library as well. Start your electronic book journey by clicking here.

Laptop Loan

A fact – a Computer Scientist cannot work without a computer. Dragging your gaming PC from home is not an option, so pick up a stylish laptop from a Laptop Loan with an engraved University logo.
