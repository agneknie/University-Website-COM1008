Friday – A gust of creativity

Friday is truly an amazing day – not only it is the end of the week, but it is also the day I have the fewest lectures! I usually spend this time dedicated to my hobbies (this is a word I use for procrastination which keeps me from studying).

So today I decided to finally edit my videos from my most recent trip. I decided to make a documentary vibe short video of our vacation from last year but I was disappointed to realise that I do not have a proper quality microphone to record my voiceover. I was about to study when I suddenly remembered that we have Media Booths on the 4th Floor of The Diamond!

Now I can proudly say – I have made the best documentary about Amsterdam from my barely usable footage from my trip. The only question left is – When is my Oscar nomination coming?
