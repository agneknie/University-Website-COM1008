Thursday – Silence is a virtue

As you may know Wednesday is known as a Sports Day – the majority of the games take place on Wednesday afternoons. Our team won! Not sure which team exactly because as a true Computer Scientist I am not interested in sports at all but my super athletic flatmates made sure that I know by partying all night.

Of course next morning I felt terrible and still heard some deep beats coming from the kitchen, so I decided to abandon my room and headed straight to the Big Silent Study on the 4th Floor of The Diamond. The fact that I made a choice of not only studying but also leaving my room was a huge achievement, so naturally I felt really proud of myself when I found a cosy, silent place in a usually crammed Silent Study. Well maybe even too cosy and too silent – I woke up on my desk an hour later.
