Monday – A lost laptop and a new found hope

Mondays truly are the worst day of the week. This morning I spilled my coffee on my laptop keyboard and of course the poor thing could not take any more punishment – it stopped working entirely. As I made my way to The Diamond through the rain and hills of Sheffield I got completely soaking wet and I am sure that it did not help my already broken laptop.

Fortunately, the people at the CiCS help desk on the 4th Floor of The Diamond were not affected by the Monday blues and were really helpful – they took my laptop and promised to repair it in the next few days. Staying without my computer even for 10 minutes gives me chills through my spine, so I was obviously terrified none the less. Thank god I remembered about the 4th Floor Laptop Loan service – I was sitting in the Flexi Space in no time, scrolling through Stack Overflow, hoping to find a solution why my images on my webpage are not aligning as I want them to.

Maybe Mondays are not such a bad day when they are spent on the 4th Floor of The Diamond.
