Wednesday – Got any ideas?

Yesterday we had our 3rd (and last!) client meeting for gathering requirements for the software we are going to build in spring. Our clients are last year students, so they should understand how hard it is to actually implement stuff but they truly played into the character of “THE client” and asked for new and insane features in the very last meeting – because why not really.

Our group decided to meet up today to discuss how can we climb out of this requirements hole and if it is at all possible. Of course, we are meeting in a Group Study Room on the 4th Floor of The Diamond – we like to pretend that we are true engineers! Getting the booking was not easy – I heard that one of my team mates had to go on some sort of a “Study Room Black Market” to get a room there for the next day, but I did not ask any questions – I was just happy to get a chance to visit one.

The discussion itself was quite nice though – not because we decided on something in terms of our assignment but because we managed to come up with some sick Sheffessions. And I do not want to brag but one of them even got published! Definitely giving my team mates the maximum score on WebPA.
