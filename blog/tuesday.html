Tuesday – Productivity is spelled similarly to Procrastination

Tuesdays is just an annoying day – I have many lectures and labs but most of them have an hour gap in between. Usually I spend that time drinking coffee or scrolling through Instagram but today I decided to attempt to spend my time productively – I am an adult after all! I made a promise to myself, that in each gap I will go to a Short-term Silent Study and catch up on lectures or do some exercises. Here is exactly how my three gaps in between lectures went:

1) Drank coffee, because I could not open my eyes after my 9am lecture;

2) Read Sheffessions, in hopes to find my own submissions. I did not but I found some really good ones and realised that I have to step up my game;

3) Opened the exercise sheet for COM1002 but at the same time I got a notification that I got recognised as one of the top fans of Sheffessions, so sent screenshots to all my friends to brag about that top fan badge.

Hopefully my time management skills get better with time, because the Short-term Silent Study really looked like a wonderful place to catch a short revision session.
